Position - in,at,on

  • I am in a cafe.
  • She is at a office! Showing activity -She is doing work.
  • Cat sits on the.
  • I am at the door.--near the door...a specific Position
  • She will go at the cafe.----means it's a specification,she can stay in the cafe or she can stay out of cafe.or she will stay near the cafe.

location -in,at,on

  • Countries----in
  • Cites----in
  • Neighbourhood/Areas---in
  • Roads/Streets--on
  • Specific Address----at

I live in USA

I live in Newyork

I live in Queens.

The dog ran out on Aslam road

She lives in Dhaka at green road

He lives at 22 hasan park,flat 3
